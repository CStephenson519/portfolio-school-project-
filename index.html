A Little Bit About Me:

My name is Chris Stephenson, I just finished my computer applications development program at Conestoga College, and now I’m on the hunt for Co-op experience. My past has consisted of a few steady jobs in manufacturing and a few years taking a run at entrepreneurialism. I have a lot of experience, in organizing events, creating marketing materials, and promoting a company or product. I’ve experienced many situations working as part of a team, from the factory setting where I could be working in groups of 2-5 people, coming together to assembly one item. Or as lead co-ordinator organizing festivals, I assisted in hosting corporate dinner functions, taking care of setup from décor, to lighting and sound, as well as acting as sous chef in the kitchen, insuring everything is properly plated and served. I’ve also been the employer, hiring and organizing staff to work at my one-off events, like New Year’s Eve and Halloween, organizing the bartenders, security, talent and everything in between. The new skills I’ve gained from school, allow me to apply my creativity in a variety of formats like web and mobile design. My ability to work with a team, and lead when required, are all things I feel would be an asset to any company.
